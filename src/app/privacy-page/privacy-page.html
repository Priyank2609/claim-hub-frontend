ClaimHub does not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only with authorized insurers, service providers, or when required by law.

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze usage patterns, and personalize content.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
